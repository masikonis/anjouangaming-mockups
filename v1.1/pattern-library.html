05 Prose Block New

Regulatory Principles Charter

The Authority operates according to core regulatory principles designed to maintain transparency, integrity, and stability within the licensing framework. The Charter outlines the guiding principles governing the Authority's regulatory mandate and supervisory approach.

The Authority's responsibilities include the administration of Internet Gaming Licences, oversight of compliance with licence conditions, maintenance of the public licence registry, and proportionate regulatory enforcement where necessary.

The Authority operates within the scope of its regulatory mandate and does not act as a forum for resolving private commercial disputes between third parties.

06 Two-Column Prose New

Authority Overview

The Anjouan Gaming Authority is responsible for the administration and supervision of Internet Gaming Licences issued under the regulatory framework of the Autonomous Island of Anjouan. The Authority maintains a licensing structure intended to support the global digital gaming industry while promoting transparency and regulatory oversight.

The Authority administers licence applications, maintains the public licence registry, and monitors compliance with applicable licence conditions. Licensed operators are required to provide accurate operational information and maintain compliance with the terms of their licence.

Regulatory Mandate

Anjouan Gaming administers the internet gaming licensing regime established under Anjouan law. The authority is responsible for the issuance, supervision, and enforcement of internet gaming licences within its jurisdiction.

All licensed operators are subject to defined regulatory standards, ongoing compliance obligations, and supervisory oversight. The authority maintains a public register of licensed entities and publishes enforcement actions as required.

23 Accordion New

Frequently Asked Questions

The Authority issues Internet Gaming Licences in two categories: B2C Operator Licences for entities operating online gaming platforms offering services directly to players, and B2B Service Provider Licences for companies providing technology or services to licensed gaming operators.

Users may verify licences by searching the public register by licence number, company name, or domain name. Licence certificates also include a QR code that links directly to the official verification page.

Applicants are required to provide corporate formation documents, information relating to directors and beneficial owners, identification documentation, and completed application forms and declarations. Additional documentation may be requested where necessary.

Licence validity periods are defined at the time of issuance. Licences are subject to annual renewal upon payment of applicable renewal fees and continued compliance with licence conditions.

24 Tab Panel New

B2C Operator Licences are issued to entities operating online gaming platforms offering services directly to players. Operators must maintain compliance with all applicable licence conditions including responsible gaming obligations and anti-money laundering provisions.

B2B Service Provider Licences are issued to companies providing technology, platform, or support services to licensed gaming operators. Service providers are subject to defined compliance obligations and ongoing supervisory oversight.
