Anjouan Gaming

Internet Gaming Regulatory Authority

The designated regulatory authority for the licensing and supervision of internet gaming operations under the laws of the Autonomous Island of Anjouan.

Jurisdiction Autonomous Island of Anjouan
Regulatory Scope Internet Gaming
Licence Categories B2C & B2B

Regulatory Mandate

Anjouan Gaming administers the internet gaming licensing regime established under Anjouan law. The authority is responsible for the issuance, supervision, and enforcement of internet gaming licences within its jurisdiction.

All licensed operators are subject to defined regulatory standards, ongoing compliance obligations, and supervisory oversight. The authority maintains a public register of licensed entities and publishes enforcement actions as required.

Administered by Anjouan Licensing Services Inc.
Governing Authority Anjouan Offshore Financial Authority
Supervisory Board Anjouan Gaming Board

Jurisdiction & Legal Basis

Establishment

Anjouan Gaming operates under the authority of the Anjouan Offshore Financial Authority, established under the laws of the Autonomous Island of Anjouan, Union of the Comoros.

Regulatory Scope

The authority's regulatory scope encompasses all internet gaming activities conducted under licences issued within the jurisdiction, including both B2C and B2B operations.

Supervisory Authority

The Anjouan Gaming Board provides supervisory oversight of the internet gaming licensing regime. Licence administration is conducted by Anjouan Licensing Services Inc. as the designated administrator.

Compliance Standards

Licensed operators are required to maintain compliance with anti-money laundering provisions, responsible gaming obligations, and all applicable licence conditions throughout the licence term.
