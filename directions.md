# Homepage Visual Directions

Two directions for the Anjouan Gaming homepage. Both share the same content, navigation structure, and brand constraints — they differ in palette dominance, typographic strategy, layout structure, and spatial tone.

All directions are anchored to the logo's colour palette (deep navy #1B1464, red #C8102E, white) and must coexist with the logo's geometric, angular wordmark and heraldic shield.

---

## Direction A — "Geometric Authority"

### Concept

Dark, structured, and architecturally precise. The page reads as a modern regulatory institution that designed its own visual language from the ground up. Navy-dominant backgrounds create gravity; geometric accent elements — derived directly from the diamond and triangle motifs in the logo's letterforms — form a unique visual system of markers, dividers, and structural cues. The overall impression is closer to a well-designed government building than a website: confident, permanent, built with intention.

### Palette

- **Dominant:** Deep navy (#1B1464) — used for hero, alternating sections, and key content areas
- **Secondary:** White (#FFFFFF) — text on dark, alternating section backgrounds
- **Accent:** Red (#C8102E) — geometric accents only: triangular bullets, angled rules, diamond separators
- **Tertiary:** A single muted navy tint (~#F0F1F6) for light section backgrounds to avoid pure white monotony

The ratio is roughly 55% navy, 35% white, 10% red accent. Red never fills a surface — it marks a point, an edge, a detail.

### Typography

**Heading:** Instrument Sans (700, 800) — geometric construction with angular terminals that echo the logo's sharp letterforms. Used at large scale for section headings.

**Body:** Instrument Sans (400, 500) — same family at lighter weights for body text. Single-family approach reinforces institutional consistency.

**Monospace accent:** JetBrains Mono or similar — used sparingly for regulatory reference numbers, licence IDs, or structured data to signal precision.

Headings are set large (clamp-based fluid sizing, ~3rem–5rem for hero, ~2rem–3rem for sections). Letterspacing is slightly tightened on headings for density. Body text at 1.125rem with generous line-height (1.6).

### Layout

- **Grid:** 12-column with visible structure. Content sits in defined blocks with subtle borders or background shifts — no floating elements.
- **Hero:** Full-width navy background. Authority name and mandate statement at large scale. No imagery. A thin red geometric rule separates the header from the hero.
- **Sections:** Alternating navy/light backgrounds. Each section has a clear heading, a horizontal rule, and structured content beneath. Key regulatory areas (Framework, Licensing, Enforcement, Public Register) presented as a grid of bordered blocks.
- **Navigation:** Horizontal top bar on navy. Active item marked with a red triangular accent. Dropdown menus are structured and grid-like, not floaty.
- **Dividers:** Angled or diamond-shaped section transitions — subtle geometric breaks rather than flat horizontal lines.

### Spatial Tone

Dense but organised. Space is structural, not decorative — it separates content areas with purpose. Padding is generous within blocks but tight between related elements. The page has visible architecture: you can see the grid, the borders, the system. This density communicates substance.

### What Makes It Memorable

The geometric accent system. Small diamonds as list markers. Angled rules between sections. Triangular nav indicators. These details, derived from the logo's own design language, create something visually unique — not a template, but a designed identity.

## Direction B — "Measured Openness"

### Concept

Light, expansive, and precisely structured. White-dominant with navy used almost exclusively for type and structural anchors. The page breathes — generous vertical rhythm, large-scale typography, substantial whitespace — but underneath the openness there is visible institutional skeleton: subtle sidebar lines, thin geometric dividers, a clear hierarchical document structure. The impression is a modern European regulatory body's public-facing site: transparent, efficient, nothing performative. Authority comes from what the page chooses not to fill.

### Palette

- **Dominant:** White (#FFFFFF) and a warm off-white (#FAFAFA) — page backgrounds
- **Secondary:** Deep navy (#1B1464) — all text, structural lines, navigation background
- **Accent:** Red (#C8102E) — precise functional signals only: 2px accent on active nav, small diamond markers on key items, thin rules
- **No background fills on cards.** No coloured surfaces beyond nav and footer. Structure comes from space and lines, not colour blocks.

The ratio is roughly 75% white, 20% navy (as text/lines), 5% red (as point accents). The palette is restrained — almost monochromatic with red as punctuation.

### Typography

**Heading:** Geist (600, 700) — geometric sans-serif with clean, slightly squared proportions. Modern without being trendy. Complements the logo's angular character without mimicking it.

**Body:** Geist (400, 450) — same family, lighter weights. The single-family approach creates a unified, calm typographic surface.

No monospace, no decorative type. The type system is deliberately simple — all differentiation comes from size, weight, and space.

Headings are set very large (~4rem–6rem for hero, ~2.5rem–3.5rem for sections) with tight letterspacing. Body text at 1.125rem with 1.7 line-height. The scale contrast between heading and body does the structural work.

### Layout

- **Grid:** Narrow content column (max ~720px for text, ~1100px for structured sections) centred on the page. Generous side margins create the sense of openness.
- **Hero:** White background. Large navy heading stating the authority's mandate. Minimal — just the name, a single declarative sentence, and space. A thin red 2px rule sits beneath.
- **Sections:** Each section begins with a large heading, a thin navy hairline rule, and then content. Generous vertical spacing between sections (6rem–8rem). No background alternation — the rhythm of heading → rule → content → space creates structure.
- **Navigation:** Clean horizontal bar. Navy background, white text. Active state marked with a subtle red underline. Minimal, functional, no decorative elements.
- **Sidebar anchors:** On wider viewports, key content blocks have a thin navy vertical line on the left edge — a subtle structural cue that says "this is important" without needing a coloured background or card.

### Spatial Tone

Expansive and deliberate. The page is mostly whitespace — and that whitespace is the design. Content islands are clearly separated by large vertical gaps. Nothing is cramped. The authority doesn't need to fill space to prove substance. This openness communicates confidence and transparency.

### What Makes It Memorable

The scale contrast and silence. Enormous headings followed by precise, measured body text. Large empty intervals. Thin structural lines. The page has a rhythm more like a designed book than a website — each section is a considered spread. When everything else in the iGaming space is loud and dense, this silence is distinctive.

## Comparison

| Aspect | A: Geometric Authority | B: Measured Openness |
|--------|----------------------|---------------------|
| **Palette feel** | Dark, weighted | Light, airy |
| **Type strategy** | Single geometric sans | Single geometric sans |
| **Layout density** | Dense, grid-structured | Open, single-column |
| **Structural cues** | Borders, fills, geometric shapes | Whitespace, hairline rules |
| **Closest analogue** | Modern government building | Nordic regulatory body site |
| **Strongest brand principle** | Modern authority | Substance without promotion |
| **Risk** | Could feel heavy if not balanced | Could feel empty if content is thin |
